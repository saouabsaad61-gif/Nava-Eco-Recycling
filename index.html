Nava Eco Recycling was founded by students *Fatima Zahra* and *Khadija*. It is an innovative online company specializing in the sale of recycled products. The company is committed to offering sustainable and practical products that encourage waste reduction and environmental protection.

A portion of the company's profits is dedicated to supporting associations focused on preserving the environment, making every purchase a direct contribution to a cleaner and more sustainable future. Through Nava Eco Recycling, we aim to combine creativity and sustainability.
